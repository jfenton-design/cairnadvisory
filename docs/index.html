About Me
Jacob Fenton, Founder & CEO

I've spent 20 years building inside some of the most demanding digital businesses in the world. Not consulting them. Running them.

At Amazon, I led product management for Kindle and helped pioneer the Dash Replenishment Service — a new recurring-revenue channel built on connected devices. At CrowdRise, I ran Business Operations through its acquisition by GoFundMe. At StockX, I built a global Customer Experience program from the ground up — managing 500 million CRM messages annually, lifting brand trust from 27% to 48%, and cutting return rates nearly in half.

That experience — the real, unglamorous, cross-functional work of making businesses grow — is what I bring to Cairn. I know what it looks like when AI lands inside an organization, and what it looks like when it doesn't. The systems, the resistance, the shortcuts that work, and the ones that don't.

I founded Cairn because I kept seeing talented operators facing AI decisions they didn't sign up for, surrounded by consultants who'd never run a team, managed a CRM, or shipped a product. I've done all of it — and I can help you do it too, starting where your business actually is, not where a deck says it should be.
