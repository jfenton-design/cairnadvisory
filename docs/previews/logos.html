02 — Logo system

The mark.

A watercolor and ink illustration of a stone stack. The mark is the canonical floating asset — transparent background, full pigment, no tile, no border, no blend mode. It floats on linen, dark umber, slate, and amber alike at the same fidelity. Standing rule: ADVISORY is letter-spaced to match the exact visual width of CAIRN — in every lockup, application, and at every size.
