05 — Voice & tone

How we speak.

Cairn Advisory sounds like a senior operator who happens to know AI deeply. Direct, warm, and never academic.

01
Operators first, technology second
We lead with business outcomes. AI is a tool, not the point. Every sentence should answer: what does this mean for the business?
02
Confident without being loud
No exclamation marks. No hype. We state clearly what we do and trust the work to speak. Restraint is a form of confidence.
03
Specific over general
We name things. "Reduce churn by 20%" not "drive efficiency." Vague language is what consultants use when they don't know.
04
Warm, not casual
A partner, not a vendor. We speak like a trusted colleague — direct, honest, human — but never sloppy or overfamiliar.
In practice
Say this
"We'll build the system alongside you."
"Here's what's actually slowing you down."
"Most AI tools your team uses can already do this."
Not this
"We leverage cutting-edge AI solutions."
"Let's ideate on your digital transformation."
"The AI ecosystem is rapidly evolving."
