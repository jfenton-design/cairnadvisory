01 — Jacob Fenton

I've spent 20 years building inside some of the most demanding digital businesses in the world. Not consulting them. Running them.

At Amazon, I led product management for Kindle and helped pioneer the Dash Replenishment Service — a new recurring-revenue channel built on connected devices. At CrowdRise, I ran Business Operations through its acquisition by GoFundMe. At StockX, I built a global Customer Experience program from the ground up, managing 500 million CRM messages annually, driving brand trust from 27% to 48%, and cutting return rates nearly in half.

That experience — the real, unglamorous, cross-functional work of making businesses grow — is exactly what I bring to Cairn Advisory. I know what it looks like when AI lands inside an organization and when it doesn't. I know the systems, the resistance, the shortcuts that work, and the ones that don't.

I founded Cairn because I kept seeing talented operators facing AI decisions they didn't sign up for, surrounded by consultants who'd never run a team, managed a CRM, or shipped a product. I've done all of it. And I can help you do it too — starting with where your business actually is, not where a deck says it should be.
